The Psychology Behind the Madness

According to "experts" (mostly us after midnight)

Nostalgia Factor

Our brains are hardwired to chase childhood joy. Tiny cars = instant dopamine.

*Study conducted in our garage with 100% biased participants

Trophy Hunting

Treasure Hunts trigger the same pleasure centers as actual treasure.

*MRI scans showed identical brain activity to pirates finding gold

Tribe Mentality

Collectors aren't weird - everyone else is weird for not collecting.

*Survey of 5 collectors and their very supportive moms

Completion OCD

That one missing car from the 2007 series? Our brains won't let it go.

*Clinical diagnosis: Awesome Collector Syndrome
